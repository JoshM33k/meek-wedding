Our Story

  • April 6th, 2015 Jessie and Josh talk for the first time, about balloon animals and music, discovering they were at the same concert a few months prior.
  • April 12th, 2015 Jessie gives Josh her number!
  • April 30th, 2015 Jessie and Josh make last minute plans and go on their first date. They see the movie Avengers 2: Age of Ultron.
  • August 2015 Josh says 'I love you' first. Jessie tried to say it minutes earlier and got too nervous. She's since gotten over the butterflies.
  • October 24-26, 2015 Jessie and Josh take their first vacation together to Niagara Falls, Canada. It was Josh's first time leaving the country, and customs made him very nervous. He tried to declare his iPad!
  • August 27th, 2016 Jessie and Josh move in together. Westfield is now home!
  • September 18-19, 2016 Jessie and Josh take a weekend trip to Nashville, TN
  • November 14th, 2016 Josh proposes to Jessie in the exact spot where they first met, in front of the movie theater, after seeing the movie Arrival (which was the longest movie of Josh's life!)
  • September 16th, 2017 Jessie and Josh get married!
  • September 17-24, 2016 The honeymoon!
